Calm Noir — 5 watch directions

The wrist app, restyled to Calm Noir and rebuilt around what you chose to surface: live heart rate, elapsed time, an honest status, and on-wrist Pause / Resume / End controls. Start stays on the phone (where the bike connects). All five share the same element set — they differ in composition and emphasis.

01 Vital Ring — HR ring hero (Apple-Workout feel), elapsed beneath, two pill controls. Most "fitness-watch" of the set.  ·  02 Big Number — HR numeral is the whole screen; maximum glanceability mid-effort.  ·  03 Dual Pair — HR + Elapsed as stacked cards, the same "Featured Pairs" language as the phone Training screen.  ·  04 Timer-Led — elapsed is hero, HR a mint sub-metric (shown here in the Paused state → Resume control).  ·  05 Workout Toolbar — HR hero with round End/Pause icon controls, native watchOS control idiom.

Every direction renders the same status vocabulary (Ready / Connecting… / No signal / Paused) via the shared StatusPill, and the controls swap Pause↔Resume by phase. The idle/"waiting for the ride to start on your phone" screen and an Always-On dimmed layout (HR + time only, no buttons) are part of the build for whichever you pick.
