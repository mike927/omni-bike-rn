How this is used

With a Bluetooth heart-rate strap and no Apple Watch, the app uses Sex, Date of Birth, and Weight to compute calories from your heart rate (the Keytel formula) — meaningfully more accurate than power-based estimation. Height enables the Mifflin–St Jeor basal fallback.
