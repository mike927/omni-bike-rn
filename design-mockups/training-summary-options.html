UX shift from today's screen. The shipped summary only shows instantaneous final metrics (the last reading before you tapped Finish) — yet the database already stores per-second samples, the gear that recorded the ride, and start/end times. All four directions use that: averages + peaks instead of a single final number, a power trend from the samples, the gear that recorded it, and a tighter combined Strava + Apple Health export instead of two stacked full-width cards.
